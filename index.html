Recipes For Halloween!

Are you ready to celebrate all the scares and the terror?
Enjoy these delicious snacks right around the frightening season of Halloween!


Oreo Spiders

This is a simple snack for anytime of the season.
This could be an activity that everyone from all ages can do and is delicious!
Ingredients:
-Oreos
-Googly eye candy
-Chocolate chips
-Pretzel rods
Steps:
1 - Break pretzel rods in half and stuff into the side.
2 - Melt chocolate chips inside of the microwave untils smooth.
3 - Dab two drop of melted chocolate on each oreo. Stick googly eyes on top of oreos.
4 - Eat and enjoy!

Spooky Bellpeppers

This is a more advanced treat just in time for Halloween and carving pumpkins.
Ingredients:
-1 tablespoon of canola oil
-1 mediumn onion, chopped
-1 garlic clove, minced
-1 pound of ground beef
-1 teaspoon of salt and ground pepper
-1 1/2 cups cooked rice
-4 large orange bellpeppers
-1 can of tomtato sauce
Steps:
1 - Heat the oil in a large skillet over medium heat, add the onion and garlic and cook until they start to brown.
Add the beef, salt, pepper and then cook until the meat is no longer pink.
2 - Add the cooked rice and canned tomato sauce and stir, then remove from stove.
3 - With a sharp paring knife, cut out the Jack-o-Lantern face on the bellpepper, cutting off the top as well and scooping out the seeds.
4 - Stuff the bellpepper with the beef mixture.
5 - Eat and enjoy!

Flying Brooms

This is a simple snack for anytime of the season.
This could be an activity that everyone from all ages can do and is delicious!
Ingredients:
-1/2 cup white chocolate chips
-1 drop of red food coloring -1 drop of yellow food coloring
-A bag of Reese's peanut butter cups
-A bag of thin pretzel rods
Steps:
1 - Place the white chocolate chips in a microwave safe bowl and microwave until the chocolate is melted.
2 - Place 1 drop of red food coloring and 1 drop of yellow food coloring, and stir until an orange is achieved.
3 - Unwrap the peanut butter cups and place them upside down on a tray.
4 - Dip each pretzel rod into the melted orange chocolate and carefully press the chocolate-coated side onto each butter cup so it stands straight.
5 - Eat and enjoy!

Monster Rice Krispies

This is a simple snack for anytime of the season.
This could be an activity that everyone from all ages can do and is delicious!
Ingredients:
-Rice Krispies
-Candy melts(grab as many different colors as you wish)
-Candy eyes
Steps:
1 - Melt the candy melts in seperate bowls depending on their color.
For example, if you grabbed purple, orange, and blue. Make sure to melt each color in separate bowls!
2 - Unwrap the rice krispies and dunk each one half way into the desired candy melt.
3 - Before the candy melt dries, be swift and place the candy eyes in whatever place desired.
tip! It is recommended to dunk one rice krispie and decorate it before moving onto another one so the decorating process isn't difficult.
4 - Eat and enjoy!
